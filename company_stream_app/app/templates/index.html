Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fab Stream?

Fab Stream is our internal business platform for department communication, employee management, task assignment, and company reporting. It helps teams stay aligned by centralizing messages, updates, and work assignments in one place.

How do I find my department and teammates?

Use the Departments page to see all active teams. Each department card shows department status, lead, summary, and a preview of employees. Click a department name to see all staff and manage team tasks if you have the right access.

What happens when I am assigned a task?

Tasks are assigned by managers and appear on your dashboard. You can view your open tasks, completed tasks, and task details from your employee profile or dashboard pages.

How do I use department chat?

Visit the Chat page and select your department from the dropdown. Messages are grouped by department so teams can discuss work, updates, and reporting in the right channel.

Can I upload reports and documents?

Yes. Reports support PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, text files, and common image formats like PNG and JPG. Upload files when creating or sharing reports through the Reports page.

Who can edit departments and employee records?

Only Admin and Manager roles can add, rename, or remove departments and manage employee details. Regular employees can view their own profile, tasks, and department information.

I’m new — where should I start?

First log in and open your dashboard. Check the Departments page to find your team, and use the Chat page to join department conversations. If you have a manager role, use the admin buttons to add departments, assign tasks, or update staff records.
